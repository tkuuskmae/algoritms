Words in alphabetical order verfier

This program will allow users to check if the words and in alphabetical order.

Instructions

  1. Insert your alphabet to the input field below. Characters may be added both in upper- and lowecase. In case of repeated entries, for instance two k-s, only the first one is counted.
  2. Insert a list of words you would like to evaluate to the field below. Words can be written both in upper- and lowercase.
  3. Hit "Evalute".
  4. The answer will appear to section "Evaluation result".
